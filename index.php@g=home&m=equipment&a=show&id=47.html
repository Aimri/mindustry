Overview:

Lead smelting rotary furnace is widely used for the lead paste smelting of battery recycling plants all over the world. The lead paste, lead grid or lead ore will have reduction reaction and become crude lead. The crude lead can be sold directly or put into lead refining kettle/pot for further refining process and being casted into ingots.
