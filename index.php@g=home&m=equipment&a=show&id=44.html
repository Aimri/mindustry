Overview Specifications Features Cases

Overview:

Blast furnace, as a traditional iron smelting furnace, can be applied to process materials with high moisture content (8% max) and well-adaptable to different feed materials such as oxide ore, carbonate ore, and so on.

Nowadays, the furnace has become the best choice for the medium and small scale raw ore smelters and large-scale spent lead-acid battery recycling plants.Hot Tags: lead smelting blast furnace, China, manufacturers, customized, quotation, low price, buy discount.

Features:


  1. High heat efficiency: furnace burden heated by the waste heat of the off-gas.
  2. High recovery rate: The molten lead and slag will be separated completely in blast furnace hearth, because the blast furnace hearth has higher temperature and more obvious slag line than rotary furnace to sedimentate the lead metal from slag.
  3. Large capacity in the same operation area: 35 t/(m2·d).
  4. Low energy consumption: No other fuels required except coal.
