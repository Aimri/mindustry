About Powder Coatings

Powder coating - A material that can be applied to the surface of an object using a variety of construction techniques to form a solid film that adheres firmly, has a certain strength, and is continuous.


Powder coating mixed in a certain proportion by resin, curing agent, pigments, fillers and additives, then through melting extrusion and crushing sieve and other processes prepared.


SM Industrial in subsoil thermosetting powder coatings industry for 20 years, product range covers with epoxy, polyester, acrylate, Bonding metallic powder coating, And set up their own sample product sample library.
