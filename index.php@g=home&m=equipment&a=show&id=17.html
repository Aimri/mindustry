FRP electrolytic cell for copper EW/ER workshop

Materials:

SM Industry great attention to cell construction material selection to increase cell service life and decrease maintenance cost.

Overview:

FRP electrolytic cells are widely used in electro-refining & electro-winning industry for Pb, Zn, Cu, Mn, and so on. Since it has good anti-corrosion and permeability resistance ability, good integrality, strong shock resistance ability, light weight, good inner surface hardness and good smoothness, and is easy to clean, it is widely applied in the metallurgical field.

Specifications:

Features:


  1. Vinyl resin coating on the cell body surface(gel ≥70％);
  2. Surface mat and chopped strand mat made of fiber glass constitute FRP cell body;
  3. Good integrality, no stitching points, horizontal and longitudinal reinforced ribs with FRP coated;
  4. Good strength, strong impact resistance, and large loading capacity;
  5. FRP density is 1.8kg/cm, lighter than polymer concrete cell;
  6. Smooth and dense inner surface, easy to clean, no pollution to anode slime;
  7. Good insulation of FRP material, no transition of spurious current, no electricity leakage, and high electricity efficiency;
  8. Modular construction, easy for transportation and saving carriage, easy for installation onsite;
  9. Above 15- year service life, easy for maintenance
