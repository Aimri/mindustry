Mature stable industrial production,have more than 100 furnaces running in different countries from Asia to America and Europe.

This process is applied for crude tin alloy with Cu, Fe, and Ni contents less than a certain proportion. The main sources for the raw materials are:

  1. Crude soldering tin;
  2. Crude tin alloy of raw ore from traditional large-scale tin smelting plants;
  3. Tin and bismuth alloy;
  4. Tin base alloy with low impurities and low melting point.

Our furnace have a wide application to all kinds of metals recovery and separation,please call or email us to discuss with our metallurgy and distillation experts to find if your materials can be treated by our furnace.
