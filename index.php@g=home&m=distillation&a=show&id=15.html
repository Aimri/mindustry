Zinc Distillation Furnace To Get High purity Zinc Zn99.9995%

Source of raw materials:

This Zinc vacuum distillation furnace is the latest patented equipment

developed for the treatment of zinc alloy including hot galvanized zinc scrap and other zinc slags from smelters . 

The technology and equipment have the following advantages:


1.Environmentally friendly:no emissions,the whole material distillation, separation,condensation and collection process is completed in a closed vacuum
system,with better working condition than traditional smelter and stronger protect on worker health. 

2.High metal recovery rate,save your metal value.

3.Short process flow,you can quick turn the zinc dross into market meet  LME standard.

4.Quality reliable,you can return the furnace if it cant meet the technical requirement as agreed.
