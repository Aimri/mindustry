Description

    This series of products is by using the advanced Bonding technology equipment to produce surface layer metal powder coating with more bright optical effect, better metalic luster, high utilization of reclaimed powder, high powder spraying rate and the coating that without the defects such as black spots, yin and yang color and fingerprints.


Powder Characteristic Chemistry
  1. Chemical type：epoxy or polyester
  2. Product types：all kinds of silver powder (bright gray flash silver, black flash silver and warm gray flash silver, etc.), all kinds of gold powder (local rich gold, champagne gold and rose gold, etc.), mirror silver (imitation plating)
  3. Specific Gravity：1.2~1.9 per CBM
  4. Curing schedule(object temp.) ：200℃/10minutes
  5. Storage ：< 30℃ in dry and cool place sealed
  6. Gloss Level： High glossy/semi glossy/matt
  7. Appearance： flat, sand grain, vertical grain and all kinds of gold and silver powder
  8. Coverage： 5~6 square meters per kg at 40~90μm film thickness
  9. Film Thickness： 50~90μm

Application

    Application Bonding Metallic Powder Coatings refers to all kinds of powder coatings which contains metallic pigments (such as: copper and golden powder, silver and aluminum powder, pearlescent pigments ). Because of its colorful decorative coating effect, this series of products is very suitable for the spraying of fitness equipment, furniture and cars etc.
