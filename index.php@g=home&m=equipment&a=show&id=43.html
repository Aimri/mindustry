Spent lead acid battery breaking and separation system

Type:

Full automatic breaking and separation

 

Input size:

Smaller than 1270mm

 

Processed materials:

Spent lead acid batteries, car batteries, motorcycle batteries, truck batteries, etc.

Batteries with or without AGM

 

Application:

Automatic breaking and separation of waste batteries

Overview Specifications Features Cases

Overview:

Spent lead acid battery breaking and separation system is fully automatic environmentally friendly battery scrap recycling system.

Through the system, batteries are crushed into pieces by breaking machine, and then the lead paste will be washed in the vibrating screen into the lead paste sedimentation boat. The oversized part from the vibrating screen then enters hydraulic separator. Through hydraulic separation, PP, heavy plastic and lead grids are separated from each other. Lead paste in the sedimentation boat will be stirred into suspended state in lead slurry mixing tank and then pumped to pre-desulfurization device when it achieves an appropriate status. After desulfurization, it will be sent to filter press to remove water and be stored in pit.



Process includes:


  1. Battery unloading and storage
  2. Electrolyte collecting and filtering
  3. Battery charging
  4. Battery breaking
  5. Separating
  6. Lead plaster filter-pressing and storage
  7. Acid mist collecting and washing


Through the process, the batteries will be separated into lead paste, lead grid, PP, and heavy plastics.
