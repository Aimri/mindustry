Pb-Ag anode plate

Pb-Ag anode sheet for zinc electrowinning

Application:

Pb-Ag anode sheet was adopted in zinc electro-winning process for zinc refining plant to get high purity zinc ingot.

We as manufacturer have supplied Pb-Ag anode plate Pb-Sn-Sb anode plate to big plant like UMMC,Pasar,RCC and etc for many years.

Please call or send email to our engineer to have direct communication if you need lead alloy sheet or process suggestion for zinc electrowinning and copper electrowinning

Materials:

Lead-silver alloy,strictly per your requirement.

 

Overview Specifications Features Cases

Overview:

Lead silver alloy anode plate is widely used in Zinc EW industry for its inert property and low production cost. Pb alloy rolling plate, with uniform crystal structure, is better in anti-corrosion and mechanical performance stability.

Pb-Ag plate connects to hanger bar through double-side welding with Pb cladding, or plate plug-in welding. In order to improve the anode property, we developed the Ti-MMO anode. It has better mechanical strength, lower soluble in the acid, lower resistance, higher current with no deformation, maintainability and long life. It's used in the harsh working environment.
