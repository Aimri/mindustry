What is the Vacuum Metal Distillation Technology?

This technology is a metallurgical process under the condition of lower than 1 atmospheric pressure to ultra-high vacuum. It mainly uses the difference of boiling point and saturated vapor pressure between elements to separate, purify and refine the matter a certain temperature and vacuum.

The process of separating and purifying metals by vacuum metallurgy is a physical process, so it is a clean metallurgy technology.
