Zinc cathode stripping machine

Processed materials:

Zinc cathode

 

Application:

Strip zinc from Al cathode

Overview Specifications Features Cases

Overview:

The automatic stripping machine performs pre-peeling, receiving, conveying, stacking and weighing of zinc cathode. It mainly consists of conveyor, zinc stripping unit, braking device, intermediate transfer conveyor, stacking and conveying unit, weighing device and upward lifting device, etc.


Specifications:

Stripping system type

With robot (Optional)

Stripping capacity

200pcs/h

Washing effect

The zinc cathode surface has no acid, anode slime, CuSO4 crystal or water droplet. It meets the grade A zinc cathode based on Chinese standard.

Plate arrangement precision

The distance precision between each plate is ≤±1.5mm, and total distance precision for 62 plates ≤±2.0mm.

Features:


  1. Highly efficient and stable loading and unloading of cathodes;
  2. Operation mode allows manual intervention;
  3. The replacement of cathodes (pre-peel-failed cathode by bare cathode) are transported by automatic multifunctional crane with no need for manual operation.
  4. Automatic stripping unit and washing unit allow for operation on double-row electrolytic cells.
  5. Chain-conveyor is able to move forward and backward;
  6. Intelligent online track and timely display to zinc stripping operation;
  7. Hardware: Five major components are independent from each other and upgrading to single unit is less complicated;
  8. Software: three sub-systems with clear division of responsibility, programmed with system recovery for future upgrading and optimization;
  9. Low-noise structure;
